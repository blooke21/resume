Quick Fun Facts

Hobbies- In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, relaxing with some reading, going for a run, or having a drink with friends and family.

Badger Boy State- In 2017, I had the "honor" of attending Badger Boys State. Badger Boy State is an immersive program that gathers high school juniors for a two-week experience at a college. Participants undergo comprehensive instruction and, by the program's second week, have gained the skills to effectively manage and govern a simulated new addition to the United States, the 51st state.

As a participant in the program, each high school student was appointed to a role within the newly formed state government. I secured the position of state representative, where my initiative aimed to consolidate three counties into one. This strategic move would grant the newly formed county the combined voting strength of three, establishing a significant majority that rendered the other counties powerless in comparison.

Yet, before I could achieve my objective, I found myself ousted from my position of authority by the acting governor. He justified his action by claiming that my pursuits clashed with the notion of "fairness" or some similarly uninspiring rationale.
